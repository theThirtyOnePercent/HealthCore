\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{classit_1_1unitn_1_1healthcore_1_1business_1_1PatientService}{Patient\+Service}} is a service class that provides business logic related to patient management. 

It interacts with the Insurance\+Plan\+Repository, \mbox{\hyperlink{classit_1_1unitn_1_1healthcore_1_1business_1_1UserService}{User\+Service}}, Doctor\+Repository, and Appointment\+Repository to perform operations related to patient insurance plans, doctor information retrieval, and appointment booking. @detail This service class contains methods for updating a patient\textquotesingle{}s insurance plan, retrieving the current insurance plan, getting detailed information about a doctor, and booking appointments with doctors.
